Bali Inspired Residence

Villa Poesia

Mansion Art Home - Where Architecture Becomes a Verse
  • Location: Moira, Goa
  • Plot Size-1350 sq mtr
  • Built Area~6390 sq ft
  • 5 Bedrooms | 24X7 Security | Maintenance Service

Villa Poesia is a silent, rhythmic retreat. Named after the Portuguese word for "Poetry," this Mansion Art Home is designed for those who seek a life that flows with intention. Here, every corridor is a stanza and every view is a metaphor for the "Slow Life" we champion at Emayaa. As our Creative Head's signature project, Villa Poesia moves beyond construction into the realm of art. We have treated the tropical Goan light as our medium, ensuring the home breathes in harmony with the surrounding nature. Villa Poesia isn't just a residence; it is a life, well-written.
