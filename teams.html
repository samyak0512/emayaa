THE MINDS BEHIND EMAYAA

A synergy of heritage, heart, and high-performance operations

Mr. BK Dhingra - Chairman

The Foundation of Trust

With a distinguished career as a practicing Chartered Account and over 25 years of mastery in the real estate sector, Mr. Dhingra provides the bedrock of financial integrity upon which Emayaa is built.

He oversees the company's fiscal health and strategic growth, ensuring that every project is backed by ironclad transparency and long-term investment value.

His decades of expertise serve as our guiding compass, blending traditional wisdom with modern stability.

Ridhii Aroraa - Creative Head

The Soul of the Space

A business graduate with a lifelong passion for design, Ridhii is the visionary behind all different concepts of each of Emayaa's Projects.

As the first point of contact for our clients, she translates dreams into architectural reality, leading the design conceptualization and planning of every home.

Beyond the aesthetic, she is the architect of our internal systems, ensuring seamless communication and rigorous quarterly reporting for our board of investors.

Prathap Arora - Managing Director

The Engine of Execution

Precision, negotiation, and on-ground excellence define Prathap's leadership at Emayaa.

From the initial acquisition of prime land in Goa and Kasauli to the final brick laid on-site, he manages the day-to-day pulse of our operations.

His expertise lies in navigating complex negotiations, managing our network of elite brokers, and ensuring that our construction standards meet the "Emayaa Benchmark."

He turns ambitious blueprints into tangible, high-quality sanctuaries.

Emayaa Luxury Homes is dedicated to crafting refined living spaces that blend elegance, comfort, and thoughtful design
